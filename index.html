San Diego Zoo

The San Diego Zoo sits on 100 acres of Balboa Park, it houses over 12,000 animals of more than 680 species and subspecies. The San Diego Zoo is the most visited in the United States. Travelers have cited it as one of the best zoos in the world. This time of year the zoo is doing summer nights, where the zoo stays open later than usual with actors walking around and special preformances happening in certain locations.

Tips and Activites

  • Wear comfortable shoes lots of walking!
  • Make sure to stay hydrated if you plan on coming during the summer they also have great refreshments at concession stands around the park as well.
  • There are plenty of activites to do as well such as, Wildlife Presentations, 4D theater, Speacial preformances in certain locations during Summer Nights, Botanical Tours, Gudied Bus Tours, an Aerial Tram called the Skyfari, and if you have children they have a water location for them to cool down with some play areas.
